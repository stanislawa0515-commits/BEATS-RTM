How this idea came to life

🎙️

The Old Way

This system was designed to eliminate traditional logbooks and optimize studio management. Previously, staff manually tracked duties in paper books, which were frequently misplaced or lost.

💿

The Workspace Evolution

By replacing physical books with BEATS, offices save valuable workspace and can seamlessly track tasks down to the exact calendar date with minimal effort.

⚡

Centralized Studio Control

Additionally, there was no centralized booking or reporting mechanism for the Conti studio. Staff entered and exited without coordination. With BEATS, anyone can secure slots securely from anywhere, anytime.
